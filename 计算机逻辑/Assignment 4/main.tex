(1) $\forall x(\exists y\, P(x,y) \to Q(x)) \;\vdash\; \forall x(\forall y\, P(x,y) \to Q(x))$
$$\begin{proofbox}
\: \forall x(\exists y\, P(x,y) \to Q(x)) \= \pre \\
\[
    x_0 \: \exists y\, P(x_0,y) \to Q(x_0) \= \falle x 1 \\
    \[
        y_0 \: P(x_0,y_0) \to Q(x_0) \= \ass \\
        \[
            \: \forall y\, P(x_0,y) \= \ass \\
            \: P(x_0,y_0) \= \falle y 4 \\
            \: Q(x_0) \= \toe 3,5 \\
        \]
        \: \forall y\, P(x_0,y) \to Q(x_0) \= \toi 4\tt6 \\
    \]
    \: \forall y\, P(x_0,y) \to Q(x_0) \= \exie y 2,3\tt7 \\
\]
\: \forall x(\forall y\, P(x,y) \to Q(x)) \= \falli x 2\tt8 \\
\end{proofbox}
$$
